## RECOMMENDATION

### For a Swedish-focused reseller business with cPanel/WHM + .SE domains:

**🏆 HostUp is the clear winner** because:

1. **Only accredited .SE registrar** among all providers evaluated
2. **Swedish datacenter** (Stockholm) — critical for Swedish SEO and GDPR compliance
3. **Swedish support** — essential for Swedish customers
4. **Lowest .SE domain prices** — €9.90/yr registration, €13.50/yr renewal
5. **Full cPanel/WHM reseller** with LiteSpeed + NVMe + CloudLinux + Imunify360
6. **Strong security stack** — Imunify360, CageFS, ModSecurity, CSF, KernelCare
7. **White-label/anonymous** — can brand as your own
8. **90-day backup retention** — better than most providers

**Caveats with HostUp:**
- No free WHMCS (you'd need to provide your own, ~$16/mo for WHMCS or use free alternatives like Blesta)
- No included CDN (but can add Cloudflare free tier)
- Smaller company than ResellerClub/A2 — less brand recognition
- Pricing may be slightly higher per GB than US-based providers

### Alternative: ResellerClub if you need:
- Free WHMCS included
- Larger infrastructure / brand backing
- Domain reseller for 500+ TLDs at wholesale prices
- But you'd lose Swedish servers, Swedish support, and .SE registrar status

### Alternative: A2 Hosting if you need:
- NVMe storage on all plans
- Free WHMCS on select plans
- Amsterdam EU datacenter (close to Sweden)
- But significantly higher .SE domain prices and no Swedish servers/support
